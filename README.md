# Task-2-Data-Visualization

## Objective
To create visualizations that convey meaningful business insights using sales data.

## Tool Used
- Power BI

## Dataset
- Superstore Sales Dataset

## Visualizations
- Sum of Sales by Category
- Sales by Month
- Profit by Region
- Sales by Segment

## Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest sales
- West region is the most profitable
- Consumer segment contributes the largest share of sales
- Sales show an overall upward trend

## Outcome
Developed a Power BI dashboard that effectively communicates business insights through visual storytelling.
